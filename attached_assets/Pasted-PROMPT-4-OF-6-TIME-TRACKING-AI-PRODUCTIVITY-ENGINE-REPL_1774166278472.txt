Floating widget, fixed bottom-right. Collapsed: clock icon (36px circle). Expanded: task name, timer '00:00:00'
(monospace, 24px), Play/Pause/Stop buttons. 'Today: 0h 0m' summary (sum time_entries for today). Task name
dropdown shows 'In Progress' tasks from team_tasks. Play: start counting. Pause: freeze. Stop: insert time_entry into DB
(task_id, date, minutes, source='timer'). Add elapsed to team_tasks.actual_minutes.

Title 'Timesheets' with week selector. Table: rows = task names, columns = Mon-Sun + Total. Cells clickable to edit time
(accept '1:30' or '1.5'). Upsert time_entry per user+task+date. Row/column totals auto-sum. 'Submit Timesheet' button.
Status: Draft/Submitted/Approved. Submit disables editing.

Project, Time, Notes.
